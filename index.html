About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University College London working on multimodal artificial intelligence for early detection of oral cancer. My research focuses on integrating genetic data with medical imaging and advanced machine learning models to improve diagnosis and screening.

My background spans neuromorphic vision, event-based video analysis, and computer vision for robotics and healthcare. I have experience in data collection and annotation, model development, evaluation, and deployment across both academia and industry.

Before joining UCL, I worked as a Data Scientist in industry and completed my PhD in Artificial Intelligence at Kingston University, where I developed methods for event-based vision and generative neural models for robotic perception.
