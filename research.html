Research

Multimodal AI for Oral Cancer Detection

At UCL, I develop multimodal AI models that integrate genetic data and medical imaging to improve early detection of oral cancer. This work involves designing deep learning architectures capable of fusing heterogeneous biomedical data to support accurate prediction and diagnosis.

Event-Based Vision and Neuromorphic Perception

My PhD research focused on neuromorphic vision systems for robotic perception. I developed fused RGB-event segmentation networks, event augmentation techniques, and spiking-neuron inspired models for asynchronous visual processing. This includes work on panoptic segmentation using graph-based neural architectures.
